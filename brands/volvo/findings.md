## Distinctive finding

Volvo's configurator is architecturally unique — single long-scroll page, no step counter, no tabs, no sidebar. Cannot be described in step-count terms. Do not force the BMW/Audi framework onto it.

Volvo is the only brand showing a stock signal from the first configurator screen. All other brands surface stock at the end screen only or not at all.

Volvo has the lowest save friction in the comparison — frictionless link copy and PDF download require no login at all. Contrast with Mercedes (account required) and Audi (third-party carLOG).

## Configurator findings — 27/06/2026

### 1. Entry CTA
Homepage hero (EX30): "Saiba mais" + "Peça uma proposta" (primary, dark).
"Comprar" nav: Automóveis disponíveis em stock · Configurar · Ofertas especiais · Usados Certificados Volvo Selekt · Frotas/Empresas · Pedir Proposta.
Stock listed before configuration — same hierarchy as Mercedes.

### 2. Configurator structure
Single long-scroll page. No step counter. No tabs or sidebar. Sections scroll sequentially. Architecturally unique in comparison.

### 3. Price visibility
Visible from model selection (desde 38.632€). Present in sticky header throughout. Final screen: "Preço final da sua configuração — 38.632€ · IVA Incluído."

### 4. Stock signal
Present from first configurator screen — "Para opções com prazos de entrega mais curtos Consulte o stock disponível." Earliest stock signal in comparison. "Automóveis similares em stock" also at end screen.

### 5. Save mechanism
- "Copiar link" — frictionless, no login
- "Guardar" (heart icon) — login status not confirmed

Classification: **Lowest friction in comparison** — multiple no-login paths confirmed.
