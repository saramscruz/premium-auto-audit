## Distinctive finding

Mercedes PT uses "Compra direta online" language and an "Online Store" breadcrumb on the configurator end screen — but both resolve to dealer lead forms ("Solicitar proposta"). The gap between stated digital capability and implemented reality is the sharpest single finding in the document. Mercedes is further along in direct-to-consumer language than BMW or Audi, but has not closed the loop to a true online purchase or reservation in Portugal.

## Configurator findings — 27/06/2026

### 1. Entry CTA
Two CTAs in sticky bottom bar on CLA model page:
- "Configure o seu veículo" — secondary (white/outline)
- "Descubra os veículos disponíveis" — primary (blue filled)

Mercedes leads with stock availability as the primary action, not configuration. Distinct from BMW and Audi which lead with configuration.

### 2. Configurator structure
Sidebar navigation, 9 top-level sections — not numbered sequential steps:
Arranque rápido · Motorizações · Variantes · Exterior · Interior · Multimédia · Sistemas de assistência e segurança · Carregamento · Resumo

Variantes and Exterior each contain 4 sub-sections. Architecturally different from BMW (14 sequential steps) and Audi.

### 3. Price visibility
Visible from first screen (49.450€). Real-time update confirmed. Financing calculation ("Cálculo Financeiro") appears inline in header when paid option selected — no other brand in the audit surfaces financing this prominently within the configurator.

### 4. Stock signal
Absent during configuration. Appears at end screen only — "Veículos de stock diretamente disponíveis" with 99% similarity matching. Three stock vehicles shown with prices.

### 5. Save mechanism
"Guardar" (blue, always visible top right) → immediate full-page redirect to Mercedes me ID login wall. Email or phone required. Account creation mandatory.

Classification: **First-party account required** — highest friction save mechanism in the comparison.

Additional save path: "Introduzir OnlineCode" present on first configurator screen. Allows re-entry via a shared code without login. Functions as a frictionless share mechanism that bypasses the login wall.

### 6. Expiry warning
Not observed.

### 7. End screen CTA
Three paths at "Passo seguinte":
- "Guardar a configuração" → Mercedes me ID login wall
- "Solicitar veículo agora" → dealer lead form
- "Mostrar veículos disponíveis" → stock listing

"Ir para loja online" on stock cards leads to mercedes-benz.pt/passengercars/buy/ — breadcrumb reads "Online Store › Veículos" but sole CTA is "Solicitar proposta" (dealer lead form).

Classification: **Dealer form only. Online purchase not live in PT.**

### 8. Mobile parity
Confirmed identical at 390px. "Descubra os veículos disponíveis" (primary blue) + "Contacte-nos" (secondary). Full parity.

### 9. Paid entry point
Not observed during session.

### 10. Account requirement
Configurator completion does not require login. Save requires Mercedes me ID.
