## BMW Group Report 2024 — extracted statements

**Source:** BMW Group Report 2024, Combined Management Report

### Direct sales status
- MINI brand: direct sales live in 10 European markets as of end 2024 (Italy, Poland, Sweden, Finland, Norway, France, Austria, Belgium, Luxembourg, Germany). Five additional markets went live in early March 2025.
- BMW brand: "The introduction of direct sales for the BMW brand is planned for a later date in Europe."
- Portugal: not named in MINI rollout list. Status: not confirmed live in PT as of report date.

### Digital commerce strategy
- "Customers will soon be able to choose whether to initiate the ordering process for their vehicle with a sales partner or online."
- "The BMW Group is decisively and consistently advancing the digital sale (online sale) of vehicles."
- "Customer Channels, Digital Commerce, Connected Company" unit established within sales strategy department.
- BMW Portugal Lda. and BMW Renting (Portugal) Lda. listed as 100% subsidiaries — confirms direct presence in PT.

### Implication for document
The annual report confirms BMW brand direct sales in Europe is not yet live. Portugal is not in the MINI rollout. The i4 "Reservar" CTA observed in the configurator is a reservation function within the existing dealer model, not evidence of direct sales. The report and the observation are consistent.

## Audi Group Report 2024 — extracted statements

**Source:** Audi Group Combined Annual and Sustainability Report 2024

### Direct sales status
No direct sales launch announced. The report explicitly positions the dealer network as the primary sales channel:
- "Our global dealerships play a key role in this forward-looking approach. They are the backbone of our sales and distribution."
- "Our dealerships are the key interface to our customers, whether in relation to sales and consultancy services or after sales services."
- "The dealership is a crucial asset in the context of such change."

### Digital strategy
- Digitalization mentioned in context of vehicle features and internal processes, not direct-to-consumer sales.
- "Audi on demand rent" and "Audi on demand subscribe" — mobility-as-a-service offerings, not vehicle purchase.
- No online ordering or direct purchase language found.

### Implication for document
The Audi annual report makes no claim to direct sales or online purchase capability. The dealer-form-only end CTA observed in the configurator is fully consistent with stated strategy. Audi is the most explicit of the five brands in affirming the dealer model as primary and future.
