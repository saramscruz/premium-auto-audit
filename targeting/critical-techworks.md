# Critical TechWorks — Data Analyst · Targeting Note

This audit was built from the outside. The BMW findings are where it gets closest to the inside.

The iX3 vs i4 CTA gap is the sharpest single observation in the document: BMW has deployed a reservation CTA on the i4 but not on the iX3 — the higher-demand model, at BREAKOUT search interest by June 2026, two months after the Neue Klasse launch in Portugal. A brand that has solved the problem for one model within the same market and infrastructure is facing a deployment decision, not a capability gap. That distinction is only visible when you cross configurator observation with search demand data and product lifecycle context from the annual report.

Critical TechWorks builds the digital products and platforms that sit behind decisions like that one. The data questions this audit cannot answer from the outside — configurator completion rate by model, return visit rate segmented by save mechanism, lead submission latency post-configuration — are exactly the questions a data analyst inside BMW's digital infrastructure would be working with.

The GA4 proxy work (BigQuery, SQL, session-depth analysis across 344,640 sessions) demonstrates the analytical framework. The audit demonstrates the method. The questions it cannot answer are the job.
