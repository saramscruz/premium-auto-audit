## H3 verdict — INVALID

Volvo PT configurator end CTA is "Pedir proposta" — dealer form only. Online purchase is not live in Portugal. The thesis holds uniformly across all five brands. Volvo is not the thesis exception.

The /shop/ URL architecture (volvocars.com/pt/shop/details/...) exists on individual stock vehicle pages, confirming e-commerce infrastructure is built. The purchase action available is "Pedir Proposta" — not a checkout flow. Volvo has built the infrastructure but has not activated online purchase in PT, identical in pattern to Mercedes ("Online Store" present, purchase not live).

---

## Distinctive finding

Volvo's configurator is architecturally unique in the comparison — a single long-scroll page with no step counter, no tabs, no sidebar navigation. Every other brand uses sequential steps (BMW: 14/12), named tabs (Mercedes: 9 sections), or step-based sidebar (Audi). Volvo's scroll model cannot be described in step-count terms. This is noted as an architectural distinction, not evaluated as better or worse.

Volvo is also the only brand in the comparison to show a stock signal from the first configurator screen ("Para opções com prazos de entrega mais curtos Consulte o stock disponível"). All other brands surface stock only at the end screen or not at all during configuration.

## Checkpoint findings — 27/06/2026

### 1. Entry CTA
Homepage hero (EX30): "Saiba mais" (secondary) + "Peça uma proposta" (primary, dark filled).
"Comprar" top navigation dropdown reveals: Automóveis disponíveis em stock · Configurar · Ofertas especiais · Usados Certificados Volvo Selekt · Frotas/Empresas · Pedir Proposta.
Stock listed before configuration in nav hierarchy — same as Mercedes.

### 2. Configurator structure
Single long-scroll page. No step counter. No tabs or sidebar. Sections scroll sequentially: Escolha a sua versão → Escolha o nível → (further options below fold). Architecturally unique — do not force step-count framework onto this.

### 3. Price visibility
Visible from model selection page (desde 38.632€). Present throughout configurator in sticky header. Final screen states: "Preço final da sua configuração — 38.632€ · IVA Incluído."

### 4. Stock signal
Present from first configurator screen: "Para opções com prazos de entrega mais curtos Consulte o stock disponível." Stock link appears earlier than any other brand in comparison. "Automóveis similares em stock" also shown at end screen.

### 5. Save mechanism
Three options at end screen:
- "Copiar link" — frictionless, no login required
- "Guardar" (heart icon) — login status not confirmed
- "Faça download e imprima" — PDF download, no login required
- "Código da sua configuração" — configuration code system under Ferramentas de compras

Classification: **Frictionless link share confirmed. Login status of Guardar outstanding.**
Multiple no-login save paths available — lower friction than Mercedes (account required) and Audi (third-party carLOG).

### 6. Expiry warning
Not observed.

### 7. End screen CTA
"O seu EX30" end screen:
- "Resumo" — outline button
- "Pedir proposta" — blue filled, primary

Ferramentas de compras: Marcar um Test Drive · Encomendar Acessórios · Ofertas/Campanhas · Código da sua configuração

Individual stock vehicle pages (volvocars.com/pt/shop/): single CTA "Pedir Proposta" — dealer form. URL contains /shop/ but no purchase flow activated.

Classification: **Dealer form only. Online purchase not live in PT.**

### 8. Mobile parity
Confirmed identical at 390px. "Resumo" (outline) + "Pedir proposta" (blue, primary). Sticky bottom bar reinforces both CTAs. "Copiar link" + "Guardar" visible. Price 38.632€ · IVA Incluído. Full parity.

### 9. Paid entry point
Not observed.

### 10. Account requirement
Configurator completion does not require login. "Copiar link" and PDF download confirmed frictionless. "Guardar" login status not confirmed.
